Here are the Bidding Rules:

1. The auction uses a second-price format, meaning the highest bidder wins.
2. If there are identical bids, the first person to place the highest bid will win.
3. The final price paid by the winner will be the second-highest bid.
4. If there is only one bid, the winner will pay the amount they bid.
